Advertise Your Property

Advertise your property for rent on Australia’s leading property sites: RealEstate.com.au, Domain.com.au and Rent.com.au (or RealCommercial.com.au and CommercialRealEstate.com.au for commercial properties).

You’ll gain access to list your rental property directly on these sites, at a fraction of the cost. This tool is generally only available for real estate agents – but now also for you, as a RentBetter customer.

Get Started

Receive Enquiries

Now that your rental property listing is live, your ad will start to attract interest from prospective tenants. These will all come through your Quarter 'My Properties' dashboard, where you can easily view, sort, manage and respond to them.

Get Started

Manage Applications

Now that your rental property listing is live, your ad will start to attract interest from prospective tenants. These will all come through your Quarter 'My Properties' dashboard, where you can easily view, sort, manage and respond to them.

Conduct Tenant Checks

Always check references! Before signing your lease, run a comprehensive digital tenancy check for only $25 (or $18 if you’re a RentBetter customer).

RentBetter uses the National Tenancy Database, the market-leading solution by Equifax to check the background of your prospective tenant. This is the most extensive database of tenant information in Australia.

Get Started Run Tenant Check

Create & Sign Your Lease

Create, view, send & sign your lease agreement online via RentBetter. Easily enter your tenant’s details and the lease terms, then click a button to send a copy directly to your tenant.

If you’ve signed up for a Match, Manage & Maintain or Manage & Maintain account, both you and your tenant will be able to digitally sign your lease.

Get Started

Set-Up Your Tenant

Once you’ve selected a tenant for your rental property (or if you have an existing tenant), you can set them up on RentBetter within minutes. Here, your tenant will be able to view and sign their lease, access their rental payment schedule and more.

Get Started

Conduct a Condition Report

It’s important to conduct a condition report of your property. There are two ways that you can do this through RentBetter:
by completing a digital condition report, or
by hiring a professional to conduct an in-person inspection for you

All reports will be saved in your 'My Properties' dashboard, so you can easily refer back to them or update them at the beginning, middle and end of your lease.

Collect Bond

Track Expenses

Track all your property expenses and manage everything in one place on RentBetter. No more shoe boxes full of receipts or trying to remember when you paid a bill. It’s all available and searchable at the click of a button.

Create Tenant Bills

Generate Reports

Generate a property report for EOFY tax time – or any other time! Your rental payments and expenses will be filed to automatically populate reports whenever you need them. They’ll be with you in minutes.

Conduct Routine Inspections

by completing a digital inspection (using the RentBetter condition report template), or
by hiring a professional to conduct an in-person inspection for you

All reports will be saved in your 'My Properties' dashboard, so you can easily refer back to them or update them as required.
